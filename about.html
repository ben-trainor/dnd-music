ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This started as a little fun idea to make DnD soundtracks feel more like an adaptive video game audio experience. Building a custom dynamic audio engine, sampling a variety of key background noises, and writing original scores, this became a really feasible idea and an outlet for my programming and composition passions.

I talked to a bunch of people about the idea and got lots of feedback and ideas for key functions. I was going to put it off until I had free time, but my web design final project was pretty open-ended, so I took the opportunity to make it way more complicated than it had to be.
